Our Story

Founded in 2008, CWC Canada has grown from a small consultancy to one of Canada's most trusted immigration firms. Our founder, Shelly Sharma, recognized the need for compassionate, expert guidance in navigating Canada's complex immigration processes.

What began as a solo practice has flourished into a team of 12 licensed immigration consultants serving clients across 25 countries. We've helped over 5,000 individuals and families successfully immigrate to Canada through various programs.
